About Us

Our Student Branch exists to provide students with opportunites for:

Networking

  • Meet local industry representatives
  • Section (Newfoundland and Labrador) events

Learning

  • Technical Tours
  • "Lunch n' Learn" - show and tell for professor's research
  • Access to Distinguished Lecture Series'

Finances

  • Scholarship opportunities
  • Funding for small projects

Leadership

  • The IEEE needs leaders! Undergraduates spend up to seven semesters in the Student Branch.... that means turnover. We're always looking for new leaders to take the Branch into the future.

In addition, our Branch boasts a McNaughton Center with advanced audio measurement software, a semi-anechoic chamber and related audio equipment.

Join Us!

IEEE opens the door to opportunities that will help you develop your professional identity in IEEE's designated fields of interest: sciences, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). In addition to accessing a range of membership benefits, you'll make global connections with people who can help you along your targeted career path through IEEE's international community of members and volunteers.
